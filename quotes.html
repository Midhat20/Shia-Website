MOLA ALI QUOTES

1. Do not let your difficulties fill you with anxiety, after all it is only in the darkest nights that stars shine more brightly.

Ali Ibn Abi Talib AS

2. Nothing hurts a good soul and a kind heart more than to live amongst people who cannot understand it.

Hazrat Ali Ibn Abu-Talib A.S

3.Life consists of two days, one for you one against you. So when it's for you don't be proud or reckless, and when it's against you be patient, for both days are test for you.

Hazrat Ali Ibn Abu-Talib A.S

4. The best deed of a great man is to forgive and forget.

Ali Ibn Abi Talib A.S

5. When the world pushes you to your knees, you're in the perfect position to pray

Hazrat Ali Ibn Abu-Talib A.S

6. A graceful refusal is better than a lengthy promise.

Hazrat Ali Ibn Abu-Talib A.S

7. He who trusts the world, the world betrays him.

Ali Ibn Abi Talib AS

8. Woman is a delicate creature with strong emotions who has been created by the Almighty God to shoulder responsibility for educating society and moving toward perfection. God created woman as symbol of His own beauty and to give solace to her partner and her family.

Ali Ibn Abi Talib A.S

9. Backbiting is the attempt of one who is incapable of doing better himself.

Hazrat Ali Ibn Abu-Talib A.S

10. Dislike in yourself what you dislike in others.

Hazrat Ali Ibn Abu-Talib A.S

11. A wise man always has something to say, whereas a fool always needs to say something.

Hazrat Ali Ibn Abu-Talib A.S

12.Surely silence can sometimes be the most eloquent reply.

Ali Ibn Abi Talib AS

13.The nourishment of body is food, while the nourishment of the soul is feeding others.

Hazrat Ali Ibn Abu-Talib A.S

14. You are master of what you say until you utter it, once you deliver it, you are its captive. Preserve your tongue as you do your gold and money. One word could bring disgrace and the termination of a bliss.

Ali Ibn Abi Talib A.S

15. You must be humble, as it is one of the greatest [forms of] worship.

Ali Ibn Abi Talib A.S
